Engaging Chicago City Council

Chicago City Council consists of 50 elected aldermen and meets monthly to shape the city. The council works with Rahm Emanuel, the Mayor, and Susana Mendoza, the City Clerk.

Chicago Councilmatic tracks all things related to Chicago City Council:

  • the legislation introduced and passed
  • its various committees and the meetings they hold
  • the aldermen themselves

Chicago's 50 Wards

Citywide issues

The Mayor of Chicago proposes most of the citywide policies that pass the City Council. These include business transactions, changes to the municipal code, issuing bonds and appointments.

View citywide legislation

Local issues

At the local level, matters like land use, signs, street parking, business permits, and other resident's issues require an ordinance with the local Alderman's support to pass.
